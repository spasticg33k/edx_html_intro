Sausage Stuffing

Sweet and savory, don't leave out this delicious side on your holiday table.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground fresh pork sausage
  • 2 sweet sausage links
  • 2 hot sausage links
  • 2 medium yellow onions – diced
  • 4 celery stalks - diced
  • 1/4 cup of sweet butter
  • 1 to 2 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh rosemary
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh sage
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh thyme
  • 4 garlic cloves mashed and finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup toasted pine nuts
  • 7 cups of your favorite crusty Italian or multigrain boule day old bread cut into crouton-size cubes
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Cut bread into croutons and let stand overnight on a baking sheet (you can also toast them). Toast the pine nuts on a baking sheet in a 350-degree oven or in a sauté pan over medium heat stirring frequently.
  2. Melt butter and sauté onion and garlic until translucent. Add sausages (remove casing & break apart) and cook until browned. Add sausage mixture to the day old bread croutons in a large mixing bowl. Mix in the herbs, celery, & toasted pine nuts. Toss with the chicken stock and transfer to baking dish.
  3. Bake in 350 degree preheated oven for 45 minutes or until crunchy on top.
